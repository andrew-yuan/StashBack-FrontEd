Welcome to BackStash!

How does it work? It's simple.

BackStash takes a portion of your daily transactions and "stashes" them until the end of each month. At that time, you can choose to donate your stash to charity or take it back based on your monthly financial situation.

Donators

We all want to help those in need and support certain organizations, but often our own unpredictable lives prevent us from doing so. BackStash is a low pressure method of giving back to the community!

Charities

There are many worthy causes that people would love to support but just aren't aware of their existence. Join us today to help spread awareness for your nonprofit organization!
